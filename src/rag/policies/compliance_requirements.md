# Compliance Requirements

## Due Diligence
All vendors must pass the following checks before approval:
- Business registration verification
- Sanctions screening (OFAC, EU, UN lists)
- Beneficial ownership disclosure for contracts over $50,000

## Industry-Specific Requirements
- **IT/Software vendors**: SOC 2 Type II certification required
- **Healthcare vendors**: HIPAA Business Associate Agreement required
- **Financial vendors**: PCI DSS compliance required
- **Construction vendors**: Valid insurance and bonding required

## Ongoing Monitoring
- Annual compliance review for all active vendors
- Immediate review triggered by adverse media alerts
- Re-certification required every 3 years
