# Payment Policy

## Accepted Payment Terms
- Net 15: Small purchases under $5,000
- Net 30: Standard terms for most vendors
- Net 45: Allowed with manager approval
- Net 60: Requires VP approval
- Net 90: Not permitted without CFO exception

## Payment Methods
- ACH transfer (preferred)
- Wire transfer (international vendors)
- Corporate credit card (purchases under $2,500 only)

## Tax Requirements
- All US vendors must provide a W-9 form.
- International vendors must provide a W-8BEN form.
- Payment cannot be processed without valid tax documentation.
