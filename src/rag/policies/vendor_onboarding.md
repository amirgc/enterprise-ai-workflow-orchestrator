# Vendor Onboarding Policy

## Required Information
All new vendors must provide the following before onboarding can proceed:
- Legal company name
- Type of goods or services provided
- Payment terms (default: Net 30)
- Primary contact email
- Country of operation

## Approval Thresholds
- Vendors with payment terms longer than Net 60 require VP approval.
- International vendors (outside the US) require compliance team review.
- Vendors providing IT services or software require security assessment.

## Prohibited Vendors
- Vendors from sanctioned countries are not permitted.
- Vendors with active lawsuits against the company are blocked.
